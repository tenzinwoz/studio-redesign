The Redwood HVAC Staffing

At The Redwood, we know that economic independence is essential to survivors of violence to rebuild their lives, support their families, and remain free from abuse. The Redwood HVAC Staffing is a tuition-free program that supports women, 2-spirit, non-binary, and trans people who have been affected by gender-based violence to pursue training and a career in the HVAC (Heating, Ventilation, and Air Conditioning) industry.

The Redwood Mobile Healing Centre

The Redwood Mobile Healing Centre, housed in a custom-outfitted RV, brings anti-gender-based violence counselling and prevention services, basic health care and other complementary services to neighbourhoods throughout Toronto and the GTA.

The Redwood offers safety, healing and opportunities for growth for women and their children fleeing abuse.
